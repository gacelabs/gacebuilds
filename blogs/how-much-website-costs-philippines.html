How Much Does a Website Really Cost in the Philippines? A Complete Guide

November 20, 2025 8 min read

Confused about website pricing? You're not alone. Quotes can range from free to hundreds of thousands of pesos. Here's a transparent breakdown of what you should expect to pay and what affects the cost.

Understanding Website Costs

Website pricing in the Philippines varies wildly – from free DIY builders to agency quotes of ₱500,000+. Understanding what drives costs helps you make informed decisions and avoid overpaying.

Option 1: DIY Website Builders

Cost: ₱0 - ₱5,000/year

Platforms like Wix, Squarespace, or WordPress.com let you build websites yourself using drag-and-drop tools.

Pros:

  • Lowest cost option
  • No technical skills required
  • Quick to set up

Cons:

  • Limited customization
  • Often look generic/template-like
  • Time-consuming to learn
  • May have ads on free plans
  • Limited SEO capabilities

Best for: Personal projects, hobby sites, or very tight budgets where professional appearance isn't critical.

Option 2: Freelance Developers

Cost: ₱8,000 - ₱50,000

Individual freelancers offer affordable rates and can create custom websites.

Pros:

  • More affordable than agencies
  • Custom design possible
  • Direct communication

Cons:

  • Quality varies greatly
  • Risk of freelancer disappearing
  • Limited support after project
  • May lack certain expertise

Best for: Simple sites if you find a reliable, experienced freelancer with good reviews.

Option 3: Small Web Studios

Cost: ₱15,000 - ₱100,000

Companies like Gace Builds specialize in small business websites, offering professional quality at reasonable prices.

Pros:

  • Professional quality design
  • Reliable ongoing support
  • Understands small business needs
  • Better value than large agencies
  • Maintenance options available

Cons:

  • Higher cost than DIY or cheap freelancers
  • May have waiting lists

Best for: Small businesses that need professional, reliable websites without the agency markup.

Option 4: Full-Service Agencies

Cost: ₱50,000 - ₱500,000+

Large agencies offer comprehensive services including branding, marketing, and complex custom development.

Pros:

  • Highest quality work
  • Comprehensive services
  • Large team with various experts
  • Best for complex projects

Cons:

  • Premium pricing
  • Often overkill for small businesses
  • Longer timelines
  • Less personal attention

Best for: Large companies with complex needs and bigger budgets.

What Affects the Price?

Understanding these factors helps you compare quotes fairly:

  • Number of pages: More pages = more work = higher cost. A 5-page site costs less than a 20-page site.
  • Custom design vs. templates: Custom designs look unique but cost more. Templates are faster and cheaper.
  • Features: E-commerce, booking systems, membership areas, and custom functionality add significant cost.
  • Content: Writing content for you costs extra. Providing your own text saves money.
  • Ongoing support: Maintenance plans are usually additional monthly fees.
  • Timeline: Rush jobs may cost more.

Professional Site (10+ pages) ₱30,000 - ₱60,000 Established businesses

What's the Best Value for Small Businesses?

For most Filipino small businesses, a professionally-built website in the ₱15,000 - ₱30,000 range offers the best balance of quality, features, and affordability.

At this price point, you get:

  • Professional, custom design
  • Mobile-responsive layout
  • Basic SEO setup
  • Contact form functionality
  • Training and support

"The cheapest option is rarely the best value. Consider the long-term cost of a poor website: lost customers, damaged credibility, and the cost of rebuilding it later."
