About Gace Builds

Helping Filipino small businesses succeed in the digital world

Our Story

Gace Builds started with a simple observation: too many talented Filipino entrepreneurs were missing out on online opportunities simply because they couldn't afford professional web development services.

We saw sari-sari store owners with amazing products, skilled contractors with years of experience, and passionate professionals offering valuable services – all struggling to reach customers beyond their immediate neighborhood.

That's why we created Gace Builds. We believe every small business deserves a professional online presence, regardless of budget. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing affordable, high-quality web development services tailored specifically for Filipino small businesses.

Today, we've helped over 50 businesses across the Philippines establish their online presence, from local bakeries in Quezon City to medical clinics in Cebu. Each project teaches us something new about the unique challenges and opportunities facing Filipino entrepreneurs.
